Date: October 10, 2025

Location: Manay, Davao Oriental

Magnitude: 6.8

Source: PNA.gov.ph

New magnitude 6.8 quake hits DavOr; tsunami alert issued

A magnitude 6.8 earthquake struck off the coast of Manay, Davao Oriental Friday night, hours after a magnitude 7.4 tremor.

The Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (Phivolcs) issued a tsunami alert, saying the first waves could arrive between 7:12 p.m. to 9:12 p.m. The waves might continue for hours.

Phivolcs advised immediate evacuation to higher areas for coastal residents in Surigao del Sur, Surigao del Norte, and Davao Oriental.

It said that the temblor, which had a depth of 10 kilometers, struck at 7:12 p.m.

Intensity IV (moderately strong) was felt in Magpet, Cotabato; Davao City, Digos City, Davao del Sur; Sta. Maria, Davao Occidental; Nabunturan, Davao de Oro; Gingoog City, Misamis Oriental; Malungon, Alabel, Sarangani; Tupi, South Cotabato; and Hinunangan, Southern Leyte. (PNA)
