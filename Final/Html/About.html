QuakeAware Mindanao is an online information hub dedicated to raising earthquake awareness across the Mindanao region. Our main purpose is to provide accurate, updated, and easy-to-understand resources that can help communities stay safe.

Why Earthquake Awareness Matters

Mindanao lies near major fault lines, such as the Cotabato Fault System, the Davao River Fault, and segments of the Philippine Fault Zone. Because of this, the region experiences frequent earthquakes, some of which have caused significant damage in the past decades.

By understanding risks, knowing safety tips, and staying prepared, every Mindanaoan can help reduce injuries, panic, and property loss during strong earthquakes.
